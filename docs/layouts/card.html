    Card

    Ace in the hole

    Basic Cards

    Cards are composed by a .head, a .body, and a .foot. By default, cards have a light background but you can change it by adding the .bg-x class and/or with a subtle .gradient.

    Hello!

    I'm a default card

    Hello!

    I'm a primary card

    Hello!

    I'm a secondary card. And I have a very long text so you can notice that the other cards are adjusting their height according to mine.

    Hello!

    I'm a success card

    Hello!

    I'm a alert card

    Hello!

    Hello!

    I'm a light card

    Hello!

    I'm a dark card. And I have a very long text so you can notice that the other cards are adjusting their height according to mine.

    Notice that using cards inside a grid will make their height equally fit.
