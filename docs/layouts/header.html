    Top-Center-Bottom
    
                            
    You can split the header in 3 vertical parts:
    
                            
      
                                
    • .head (always at the top)
      • 
                                
    • .body (always vertically centered)
      • 
                                
    • .foot (always at the bottom)
      • 
                            
    
                            
    
                                
    
                                    
    
                                        
     Always at the Top 
    
                                    
    
                                
    
                                
    
                                    
    
                                        
     Always Vertically Centered 
    
                                    
    
                                
    
                                
    
                                    
    
                                        
     Always at the Bottom 
    
                                    
    
                                
    
                            
    
                            
    Tip: you can add the class .text-center in order to horizontally align items.
    
                            
    Remember that you can use .head and .foot with components and elements!
